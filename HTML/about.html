@Saad Metla

Saad Metla is a Pakistani photographer and music video director/producer based out of New York City, with a portfolio of music videos that amass millions of views.

Though originally graduating with a B.A. in Psychology, his passion in both photography and producing pushed him to explore a career path that went against the traditional South Asian norms. It has allowed him to receive credits with some of the best and brightest talent in the music industry including Fetty Wap, the Migos, Rich the Kid, Tory Lanez, and even Indian sensations Divine and Diljit Dosanjh!

Saad's approach in film-making is rooted in realism based on generational trauma, depiction of relatable characters/representation, and his intrinsic need to push empathy. In the future, he plans to explore proximity-based augmented reality advertisement and incorporate his psychology degree to study the use of virtual reality exposure therapy on trauma.

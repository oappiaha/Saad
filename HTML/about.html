Saad Metla is a Pakistani photographer and music video director/producer based out of New York City, with a portfolio of music videos that amass millions of views.

In addition to his work as a photographer and music video director/producer, Saad Metla has also taken on the role of an A&R professional. Through this role, he aims to bridge the gap between East and West by actively seeking and promoting talented South Asian artists and providing them with a platform to express their voices. Saad is dedicated to representing the South Asian diaspora and showcasing their unique perspectives and experiences through various forms of art and music.

Furthermore, Saad's commitment to his psychology degree extends beyond his interest in virtual reality exposure therapy. He incorporates his understanding of human behavior and emotions into his filmmaking process, allowing him to create authentic and relatable stories that resonate with viewers on a deep level. By addressing generational trauma and emphasizing the importance of empathy, Saad's work strives to provoke meaningful discussions and foster understanding among diverse audiences.

Looking ahead, Saad envisions exploring proximity-based augmented reality advertisement, leveraging cutting-edge technology to engage viewers in innovative ways. By incorporating his psychology background, he aims to study the impact of virtual reality exposure therapy on trauma and contribute to the advancement of therapeutic approaches in mental health.
